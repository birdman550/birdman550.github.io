Hi, I'm Sanket Deshpande

I'm a final year student at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani)- Goa;
pursuing dual degrees in MSc. Physics and BE Electrical and Electronics Engineering.
Currently, I'm working on my thesis project with the India-based Neutrino Observatory (INO) group at
Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) and also leading the development of a nanosatellite constellation as the Chief Operations Officer at Pixxel.

I'm passionate about engineering projects which can positively impact society.
Over 4 years of my undergraduate studies, I led a team to conduct India's first student-led near-space mission,
helped found a Braille-tech startup, developed a hyperloop pod and gave a TEDx talk. Some interesting pictures of this time:
