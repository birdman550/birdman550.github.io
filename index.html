Hi, I'm Sanket Deshpande

I'm an Embedded Systems Engineer at Atomionics, where we're buiding
cold-atom interferometry based sensing and navigation systems.
I studied Physics and Electrical Engineering at the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani university in India.

I'm passionate about solving problems involving a confluence of Physics and Engineering.
Previously, I led a team to conduct India's first student-led near-space mission, built an airborne
NOx measuring instrument with NASA LaRC, helped found a Braille-tech startup, and developed a hyperloop pod.
