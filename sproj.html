Project Apeiro

Project Apeiro is an experiment to determine cosmic ray flux in the lower stratospheric regions of the atmosphere. It was conducted by deploying a cosmic ray detection system on a High Altitude Balloon (HAB). The flight was successfully conducted on 2nd February, 2018.

I started working on this project in my first year of undergraduate education. We were a group of freshmen highly motivated work on "some" space project. But, starting a student satellite mission seemed like an uphill task which might have taken more time than the duration of our education. After a lot of searching, we arrived at the idea of developing a near space experiment. This allowed us to work on a project with similar complexities of a space mission while saving us from the lengthy and beauracratic process of a space mission.
